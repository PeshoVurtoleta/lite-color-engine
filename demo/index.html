Accuracy Comparison

The same vivid OKLCH gradient packed three ways. FAST substitutes sqrt for the sRGB transfer (~10/255 midtone drift). ACCURATE uses the true transfer with a hard gamut clamp. GAMUT-MAPPED runs MINDE chroma reduction (from the /gamut subpath) to preserve hue near the gamut boundary.
