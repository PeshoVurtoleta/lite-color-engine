Accuracy Tiers

One vivid OKLCH sweep packed three ways. FAST swaps a sqrt for the transfer (~10/255 drift). ACCURATE uses the true transfer with a hard clamp. GAMUT runs MINDE chroma reduction from the /gamut subpath to hold hue near the boundary.
